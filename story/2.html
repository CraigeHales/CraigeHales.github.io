Saturday, 6:00 AM

About an hour later, Charles rested for a moment and thought about removing his jacket. He was nibbling on a bit of grass and thinking about how his sibblings were waking up and about what they might get for breakfast. "It is getting warm, or maybe the brisk walk is making me hot", he said, under his breath. And with that, he wriggled out of the jacket and stuffed it into the satchel.
